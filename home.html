Waste Management

Waste isn't waste untill we waste!!!

It is necessary for every individual of community to understand his/her responsibility towards waste management and to understand that it is not just the duty of administration to manage waste because environment is shared by all.

So be a responsible citizen and regsiter a pickup request whenever you feel the garbage-can in your locality is full.

Mis-Management of Waste

Improper waste disposal is the disposal of waste in a way that has negative consequences for the environment. Examples include littering, hazardous waste that is dumped into the ground and not recycling items that should be recycled.

So register a complaint whenever you witness mismanagement of waste or for any other waste management related issues.
